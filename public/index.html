Welcome to Pantry!

This app will help you keep track of what food you have in your home - who put it there, when they put it there, how much is left, etc. Users will create a "Pantry", which can be a specific refrigerator, cabinent, shelf, or location that you store food.

After you create your Pantry, you will be able to add items, edit them, and remove them when they are gone.

To get started tracking your food items, click sign up above!
